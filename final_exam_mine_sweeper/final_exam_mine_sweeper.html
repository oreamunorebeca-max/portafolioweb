How to Play

  • Controls: Use your Arrow Keys to navigate the board.
  • Collect: Reach the items to increase your score and advance to the next level.
  • Avoid: Stay away from the bombs!
  • Lives: You have 3 lives. If you hit a bomb 3 times, it's Game Over.
